Every summer, Girls Who Code, a non-profit organization dedicated to reaching gender parity in tech through education, holds their Summer Immersion Program. For 7 weeks, classrooms across the country filled with rising high school juniors and seniors go through an intensive, hands-on curriculum involving lots of coding and an introduction to Computer Science. These are the students in the Newark, NJ classrooms hosted at NJIT and sponsored by Verizon. Click through to get a brief insight into their experience.
